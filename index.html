Rock Paper Scissors

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game!

How to Play:

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played between two players. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand:

  • Rock: Shown by forming a fist.
  • Paper: Shown by an open hand.
  • Scissors: Shown by extending the index and middle fingers.

To determine the winner:

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Paper beats Rock.
  • Scissors beats Paper.

The game is usually played by counting to three and then simultaneously revealing the chosen shape. The winner is determined based on the rules mentioned above.
